## 2) Jezyk angielski (opcjonalnie)

### App name

`AquaMind - Aquarium Assistant`

### Short description (max 80 characters)

`Track water tests, history, and aquarium insights in one app.`

### Full description

`AquaMind helps you manage your aquarium with clear data and practical insights.`

`Log measurements, review trends, and spot potential issues before they become serious.`

`What you can do:`
- `manage multiple tanks`
- `save water test results (including pH, GH, KH, NO2, NO3, NH3/NH4, PO4, Fe, Ca, Mg, temperature)`
- `review measurement history and current tank status`
- `manage fish and plant stock`
- `get analysis and recommendations based on your data`

`Why AquaMind:`
- `clean and focused interface`
- `account-based data storage with backend security rules`
- `in-app account and data deletion flow`

`Important: AquaMind provides informational support. For serious fish or plant health issues, consult a qualified expert.`

### What's new

`First production Android release of AquaMind.`
- `user account and sign-in`
- `multi-tank support and measurement history`
- `parameter analysis and guidance`
- `stock management`
- `full account and data deletion flow`
